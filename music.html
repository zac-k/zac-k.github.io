Music

Just some of my music—all produced using softsynths (Spire is my current go-to) and a DAW (usually Bitwig at the moment). Stylistically, most of my music falls under the broad umbrella of "trance", but I've been branching out a bit lately with some house, drum and bass, and other genres (not all posted yet). Except where otherwise stated, vocals are from royalty-free sample packs. Hover over a track to see my own thoughts about it.
