Music

Just some of my music—all produced using softsynths and a DAW (usually Bitwig at the moment). Stylistically, most of my music falls under the broad umbrella of "trance". Some of it is generic 90s to early 2000s style hard (anthem) trance, some it closer to psy trance, and some is a bit slower with house and/or funk/electro elements. I've been branching out a bit lately with some house, drum and bass, and other genres (not all posted yet).

I'm not a huge fan of techno, but I decided to give it a try. While I definitely managed to incorporate some quintessential techno elements into Arte 7, I think my propensity for Trance production comes through pretty strongly. Not sure how a Techno aficionado would categorise it. It's difficult to know what genre would best describe Sphere of Interest. It probably qualifies mostly as trance, but it also has some big room elements, so some people might think it fits the "house" label better. As it's quite different to the tracks I have listed under "hard trance", I have decided to put it in "other".
