I am a theoretical/computational physicist with a PhD from Monash University. I have a passion for anything, creative or technical, that utilises computers and other technology. My PhD research examined the accuracy in the reconstruction of three dimensional electromagnetic vector fields using transmission electron microscopy. For this work, I developed skills in the production of highly realistic electron-optical simulations.

If you're currently hiring, and think that I may have the skills you're after, feel free to check out my CV and browse my github repositories.
