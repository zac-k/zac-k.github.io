Used to Have It was the first House track I produced that I was really, really happy with. It's got a super chill, jazzy vibe, and a kind of 'organic' and spacious feel. There's nothing about this track that I'm unhappy with. I love the funky bassline, and the synthesised saxophone sounds pretty authentic to me, which gives the track a recorded instrument feeling.
