Repository, in my opinion, is the most polished of my hard trance anthems. It's got a great, uplifting chord structure and melody, and exemplifies the style.
Elegant Burn is another solid trance anthem. Less of an uplifting vibe than Repository, and more of a rough energy to it.
Discovery 7 is less 'radio-friendly' than the previous two tracks, but it's one of my favourite. Although it's still firmly in the hard trance genre, the beat leans slightly towards a disco sound. For me, it evokes 80's futurism imagery. I feel I managed to achieve the intensity of hard trance without compromising on the feel I was going for.

Sublimation is great, if somewhat generic hard trance. Long build up, leading to a beautiful anthem.
Tertium Septendecim is another hard trance anthem with a long build up. The melody is less emotional than than those in most of the other anthems, but has an upbeat vibe and plenty of energy.
Recrudescence is yet another generic anthem, but with a shorter build-up to the drop than the previous two tracks.
I personally love Voyager 7. The pace is slower than most of my other trance tracks, which allows for a different mood. This is a really cohesive track that manages to achieve a professional sound without any compromises on creativity.
Omicron is an intense and high energy track.
ht5 is one of my older trance tracks (from around the late 00's). It's got more of an old-school vibe to it. I love the melody, and have always been happy with the track over all, but my technical skills have progressed signifactly since I made this, and there are things I would do differently if I was producing it today.
